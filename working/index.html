Instructions

  1. Click the space button to start playing.
  2. The ball starts to move across the board. Click the space key when the ball is inside the blue box.
  3. Your life is represented in the bar on the bottom-left corner. If you miss the box- you loose life.
  4. If you caught the ball inside the box- you receive 1 point (see on top left). In addition, the speed of the ball is increased.
  5. Make sure to receive as many points as possible before your life runs out.
  6. Good luck!!
